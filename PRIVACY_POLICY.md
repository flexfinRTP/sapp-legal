# Privacy Policy for Genesis - Sober & Free

**Last Updated: December 2025**

## Overview

Genesis - Sober & Free ("we," "our," or "the app") is a privacy-first recovery and wellness tracking application designed to help users track their sobriety, monitor health metrics, and build better habits. This Privacy Policy explains our commitment to protecting your privacy.

**Genesis is 100% free** with all features unlocked. No ads, no subscriptions, no catch.

## Our Core Privacy Principles

**We Do Not Collect Your Data. Period.**

Genesis is built with privacy as its foundation. Unlike most apps, we:
- **DO NOT** collect any personal information
- **DO NOT** track your usage or behavior
- **DO NOT** share your data with anyone
- **DO NOT** use analytics or telemetry
- **DO NOT** require an account or login
- **DO NOT** connect to the internet for core functionality

## Data Storage and Control

### Local Storage Only
- **All data remains on your device** - Your recovery progress, health metrics, goals, and personal data are stored exclusively in your device's local storage
- **No cloud backup** - We do not maintain servers or cloud storage for your data
- **No synchronization** - Data is not synced to any external service
- **Complete control** - You can delete all your data at any time from the app's settings

### Data You Can Track (All Stored Locally)
- Sobriety/clean time streaks for 17 addiction types
- Craving logs and HALT trigger analysis
- Weight and BMI measurements
- Exercise plans and workout schedules
- Personal goals and recovery milestones
- Notes and timestamps

## Permissions

### What We DON'T Request
Genesis explicitly **blocks** the following permissions:
- ❌ Camera access
- ❌ Microphone access
- ❌ Location services (GPS)
- ❌ Contacts access
- ❌ Phone state or call logs
- ❌ SMS or messaging

### What We DO Use
- ✅ **File Storage** - Only to allow you to export/import your own data as backup files
- ✅ **File Sharing** - Only to let you save backup files or share achievement cards

## Data Export and Backup

### Your Data, Your Control
- You can export all your data at any time as an encrypted backup file
- Exports are encrypted and obfuscated for your security
- You control where backup files are saved
- Imports allow you to restore your data or transfer to a new device
- Export/import works entirely offline

### No Data Transmission
- Export files are created and saved locally on your device
- No data is transmitted to our servers (because we don't have any)
- Backup files remain in your control

**Genesis is completely free.** There are:
- ❌ No subscriptions
- ❌ No in-app purchases
- ❌ No premium tiers
- ❌ No payment processing
- ❌ No ads - ever

All features are unlocked from day one. Recovery shouldn't have a paywall.

## Third-Party Services

### External Resources Only
Genesis provides links to external resources for:
- Emergency hotlines and crisis support services
- AA/NA meeting finder resources

**Important:** When you click these links or dial phone numbers, you leave the app and interact directly with those third-party services. We have no control over and are not responsible for the privacy practices of external services.

### No Third-Party SDKs
We do not integrate:
- ❌ Advertising networks
- ❌ Analytics platforms (like Google Analytics or Firebase)
- ❌ Social media SDKs
- ❌ Marketing tools
- ❌ Data brokers

## Not a Medical Device or Service

### Lifestyle Tool Only
Genesis is a **personal lifestyle tracking tool** and is **NOT**:
- ❌ A medical device
- ❌ Medical advice or treatment
- ❌ A substitute for professional healthcare or addiction treatment
- ❌ Intended to diagnose, cure, or prevent any disease
- ❌ A crisis intervention service

### Disclaimer
The information and tools provided in Genesis are for general informational and personal tracking purposes only. Always seek the advice of qualified healthcare professionals for addiction treatment and mental health concerns. If you are experiencing a medical emergency or mental health crisis, contact emergency services immediately.

## Children's Privacy

Genesis is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since we don't collect any data, this is inherently satisfied, but parents should supervise their children's app usage.

## Data Retention and Deletion

### Your Choice
- **Retention:** Data stays on your device until you delete it
- **Deletion:** You can delete all app data instantly from Settings → Reset All Data
- **Uninstall:** Uninstalling the app removes all local data permanently

### No Recovery
Once you delete data or uninstall the app, we cannot recover it because we never had access to it in the first place.
